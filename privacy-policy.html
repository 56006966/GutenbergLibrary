Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Phunky Pixels respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Project Gutenberg Library app, its related Project Gutenberg catalog and mirror services, and related Project Gutenberg Library pages operated by Phunky Pixels.

Project Gutenberg Library is designed to let users browse, download, and read public domain books. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we and the servers the app connects to may process limited technical information needed to operate the service, including:

  • your IP address
  • basic request and server log data
  • app requests for book metadata, cover images, and book files
  • locally stored reading data such as downloaded books, favorites, and reading position

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, home address, or payment information through the app.

How Information Is Used

Technical and log data may be used for:

  • providing access to book catalogs, cover images, and book files
  • maintaining service reliability
  • troubleshooting errors
  • quality assurance
  • aggregate usage analysis

Locally stored reading data is used only to support app features such as offline reading, favorites, and resume position.

Analytics and Tracking

This app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs such as Google Analytics, and does not intentionally include third-party tracking technologies.

Log Retention

Server access logs may include your IP address and request details. These logs may be retained temporarily for service operations, diagnostics, and abuse prevention. Log data may be automatically deleted after a limited retention period.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The mobile app itself does not use browser cookies in the normal course of use. However, if app content is opened in a web view or external browser, the websites you visit may use cookies, captchas, or similar technologies under their own policies.

Downloaded Books and Local Storage

The app may store the following information on your device:

  • downloaded books and EPUB files
  • cached cover images
  • favorites
  • reading progress and last-read position
  • display preferences such as text size or theme

This information remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear its data.

Third-Party Links and Content Sources

The app may access or display content from Project Gutenberg, mirror servers, or other external websites. When you open or follow a third-party link, the privacy practices of that external site apply. Phunky Pixels does not control those third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
